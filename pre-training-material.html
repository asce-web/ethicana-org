Pre-Training Material

The Train-the-Trainer Kit is primarily designed for the novice trainer, but portions of it are of benefit to experienced trainers.
Including three separate options for flexibility, the kit provides all the content necessary for two hours of training based on the Global Anti-Corruption Education & Training Initiative (ACET) materials. Each option for instruction is designed for working in tandem with the 42-minute Ethicana movie. To view and download the materials, click on the links at left.

Option B: On-site viewing of "Ethicana" based on the 54 Issues for Discussion
Option C: On-site viewing of "Ethicana" with discussion based on the full training packet
