Post-Training Material

Resources for use following completion of the "Ethicana" training are available from the links at left.

Download the assessment form to copy and give to trainees to seek their feedback. The form allows participants to grade the success of the training, as well as provide written, individual responses. The results will help the developers of "Ethicana" plan any future revisions.

Also available for review is the complete "Ethicana" script. Once the film has been viewed prior to training, the script may be printed out to allow highlighting of scenes and lines that make for good discussion.
